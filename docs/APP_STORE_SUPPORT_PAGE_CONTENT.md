## App Store Connect – Support URL

1. Create a new Notion page and paste the content above (or your version).
2. Click **Share** → **Publish to web** → turn **Publish** on.
3. Copy the published URL (e.g. `https://xxx.notion.site/...`).
4. In App Store Connect → your app → **App Information** → **Support URL**, paste that URL and save.

**Note:** Support URL must be a valid `https://` link. You cannot use only an email address.

## Copyright field in App Store Connect

**Yes, you can use your company/brand name (e.g. mimoom) for Copyright.**

- Example (same as your previous app): **`© 2025 mimoom`**
- Use the legal or brand name of the rights holder. No special format is required beyond the year and the name.
